Bachelor — a design diploma in CAD Kompas 3D.

By the 6th year I realized I was far more drawn to computers than to the factory)  
HH.ru — an invitation to an IT Institute.

Thesis: _“Analysis of large datasets from the ForeMan system and a predictive algorithm for forecasting the 'survivability' of machines and equipment in real time”_

That's how my journey into development began — and I never regretted it!
